In-Depth Visual Design

For my topic I want to discuss Visual Design and the topics from it.

Elements of Visual Design

There are many different elements stated by Teo Yu Siang. Some of these include Lines, Shapes, Negative Space, Volume, and Texture.

Visual Design Principles

There are many different principles stated by Teo Yu Siang. Some of these include Scale, Contrast, Balance, Hierarchy, and Gestalt.

In this example I want to break down some of the aspects of the FireFox start screen.

Dominance

The Fire Fox Logo takes up the majority of the screen. This makes it the top priority/main foucs of the screen.

Unity

Each option at the bottom is equally spaced and gives an equal feel to the selections.

Shape

The search bar is rectangular and easy to use. While the rest of the screen utlizes simple shapes to noy put more cognative load on the user.

Negative Space

Almost the entire screen is negative space. This lets the Logo pop out at the user.

Visual Design in UX

The five Priciples discussed by Kelley Gordon are

  • Scale
  • Visual hierarchy
  • Balance

  • Gestalt

In the article she discusses that if these principles are utlized well, it could increase brand perception, usability, and provoke emtion and delight.
